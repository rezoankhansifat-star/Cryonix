As mentioned above, the housing unit consists of 3 modules: Housing Module, Laboratory and Transition Module.
The Laboratory and Housing module, are, outwardly, equal, only differ in the arrangement of the partition walls. Each of these modules consists of a cylinder with 14 meters in diameter and 2 meters high. On top of this cylinder is a spherical dome with a maximum height of 6 meters. The displacement module is only a cylinder with a radius of 2.4 meters, cut and lying down, which joins the laboratory module with the housing module.

The goal of building a structure on Mars is to create a shelter for a team of 4 . The housing unit needs to ensure the safety of the team facing the Mars adversities and to ensure that the mental health of astronauts is not compromised. For this purpose, the design of the housing unit on Mars will consist of 3 elements with different objectives and will be built using mainly technology associated with 3D printing:
* Laboratory and Work Module;
* Transition Module - House - Work;
* Housing Module - Leisure.

The sizes of the compartments within the Lunar Surface Habitat (SH) are defined by the required habitability functions, using the recommended minimal volume and dimensions for those functions as a basis for the internal layout. The resulting areas and volumes in the final layout generally tend to be larger than the recommended minimums.
The overall Surface Habitat layout is designed for two crewmembers for 30 days, and includes three internal levels. The total habitable volume in the layout is significantly larger than the recommended minimum.
Overall Habitat and Level Dimensions
The standard height for most volumes is 2.4 meters, excluding the Sleep Accommodation, Temporary EVA Items Stowage, and Airlock Functions.

The habitat is divided into three levels with specific floor heights and areas:
• Level 1 (Airlock, Work Surface, EVA Computer, ECLSS):
    ◦ Area: 33.2 m².
    ◦ Floor Height: 2.5 m.
• Level 2 (Hygiene, UWMS, Exercise, Utilization, ECLSS):
    ◦ Area: 17.2 m².
    ◦ Floor Height: 2.5 m.
• Level 3 (Crew Quarters, Galley, Ward Table, Utilization, Storage, ECLSS):
    ◦ Area: 15.2 m².
    ◦ Floor Height: 2.6 m.
Crew Translation Paths are standardized at 1.00m wide.
